Furniture Design · 2025

Neo-Chinese Furniture Collection

A curated collection of seating and tables exploring the intersection of Eastern tradition and contemporary craftsmanship. Spanning four design schemes — from the bold ink-wash tea chair set to sculptural circle-back armchairs with gilded bamboo joints, minimalist Nordic-inspired lounges, and a serene Zen wood series with lattice backs and rattan seats. Each piece balances structural integrity with poetic restraint.

Industrial Design · 2024

EcoBin Family Waste Sorting Robot

A smart home companion that makes waste classification effortless. EcoBin combines AI-powered sorting with a friendly, approachable form factor. The project features detailed 3D rendering, hand-drawn technical sketches, exploded part diagrams, and atmospheric scene compositions that place the product in real home environments.

Brand VI & IP Design · 2025

YOUNG INN'S FIRE PUB · 漾应的火塘

A complete brand identity built around the warmth of gathering. The core IP — a cat with a flame-tipped tail — embodies the brand's promise: "Around the fire, real friends gather." The VI manual includes the mascot's three-view drawings, application mockups, and a comic strip that brings the character to life through storytelling about traditional tie-dye craftsmanship.

Brand VI & Spatial Design · 2025

YOUNG INN'S COLOR · 漾应的色

A plant-dye lifestyle brand with a Mondrian-inspired geometric VI system. The project encompasses the full brand toolkit — packaging, gift boxes, stationery — alongside a complete interior and exterior spatial design for the flagship store. The space blends Japanese Zen aesthetics with modern minimalist retail design, featuring plant-dye workshops and Instagram-worthy outdoor installations including a giant cat sculpture.
